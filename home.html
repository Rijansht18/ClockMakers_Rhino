Yoga Pose

Mountain Pose (Tadasana)

Steps to Perform

  • Step 1: Stand with your feet together or hip-width apart, grounding through all four corners of your feet.
  • Step 2: Engage your thighs, lift your kneecaps, and draw your belly inward.
  • Step 3: Roll your shoulders back and down, keeping your chest open.
  • Step 4: Let your arms rest alongside your body with palms facing forward.
  • Step 5: Lengthen your spine by lifting the crown of your head toward the ceiling.
  • Step 6: Breathe deeply, maintaining a steady and relaxed posture.
  • Step 7: Hold the pose for 30 seconds to 1 minute while focusing on your breath.

Physical Exercises

Burpees

Steps to Perform

  • Step 1: Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and arms by your sides.
  • Step 2: Drop into a squat position and place your hands on the floor in front of you.
  • Step 3: Kick your feet back into a plank position while keeping your body in a straight line from head to heels.
  • Step 4: Perform a push-up (optional) by lowering your chest to the ground and then pushing back up to the plank position.
  • Step 5: Jump your feet forward, returning to the squat position with your hands still on the floor.
  • Step 6: Explosively jump into the air, reaching your arms overhead, and land softly back into the squat position.
  • Step 7: Repeat for the desired number of reps, maintaining a steady pace.
